The secret to being busy - but in the right ways.

One might call the college experience the ultimate balancing act; a battle of academia, social life and first-time independence. But just as students may be at risk of overcommitment, they can also be prone to self-isolation.

Cal Poly students of all differnet majors have taken it upon themselves to find ways to keep themselves busy, but in ways that are healthy and fulfilling.

Hobby Prioritization

Passionate Pastimes

The college adaptation process varies by student, but four Cal Poly students individually agree that having a hobby or pastime has made their experience more fluid and manageable. Their emotional wellbeing is stronger, their lives are more balanced and there is a comfort that comes with maintaining their passions outside of their academic work, they say — without their hobbies, their college lifestyles would be vastly different and less fulfilling.
